# Remindwise

**Never forget life's recurring tasks.** Version 1.0.0 - iOS and Android.

Remindwise remembers the things that come back every few weeks, months or
years and quietly slip your mind: replacing the toothbrush, servicing the
boiler, booking the MOT, the flea treatment, the Christmas gift list.

## What it does

- A library of 195 ready-made tasks with realistic default frequencies,
  organised in categories, plus starter packs (car owner, home owner, renter,
  parent, pets, garden, freelancer and more) and a 60-second setup interview.
- Schedules that match real life: every X days/weeks/months/years, a fixed
  day of the month, the Nth weekday of the month, or counting back from a
  date ("8 weeks before Christmas", "1 week before the MOT"). Complete a task
  late and the schedule restarts from the day you actually did it.
- One gentle notification on the due day, at the time you choose, with Done
  and Snooze actions right on the notification.
- A streak of days with nothing overdue, a monthly calendar and statistics.
- English and French, switchable in the app.

## Free and Pro

The core app is free, forever, with no ads and no account. Remindwise Pro
(subscription or one-time lifetime purchase) adds: unlimited active
reminders, full statistics with a shareable performance card, unlimited
custom categories, appearance themes and per-task notification times.

## Privacy Policy

Effective date: 11 June 2026 - App version 1.0.0

**The short version: Remindwise collects no data. Everything stays on your
device.**

**Data collection.** Remindwise does not collect, transmit, sell or share any
personal data. There is no account, no sign-in, no analytics SDK, no
advertising SDK and no tracking of any kind.

**Your content.** The tasks, categories, history and settings you create are
stored only on your device. They are included in your device's standard
backup mechanism (Android Auto Backup / iOS device backup to iCloud), which
is operated by Google/Apple under your own device settings. We have no access
to it.

**Notifications.** Reminders are local notifications scheduled on your
device. No notification data leaves your device.

**Purchases.** Optional Remindwise Pro purchases are processed entirely by
Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We never receive your payment
details. Purchase entitlement is stored on your device and with your store
account for restore purposes.

**Children.** Remindwise is a general-audience app and does not knowingly
collect any data from anyone, including children.

**Changes.** If a future version ever changes any of the above, this policy
will be updated and the change clearly announced in the app's release notes
before it takes effect.
