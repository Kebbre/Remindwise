# Remindwise

**Never forget life's recurring tasks.** Version 1.0.0 - iOS and Android.

Remindwise remembers the things that come back every few weeks, months or
years and quietly slip your mind: replacing the toothbrush, servicing the
boiler, booking the MOT, the flea treatment, the Christmas gift list.

## What it does

- A library of 195 ready-made tasks with realistic default frequencies,
  organised in categories, plus starter packs (car owner, home owner, renter,
  parent, pets, garden, freelancer and more) and a 60-second setup interview.
- Schedules that match real life: every X days/weeks/months/years, a fixed
  day of the month, the Nth weekday of the month, or counting back from a
  date ("8 weeks before Christmas", "1 week before the MOT"). Complete a task
  late and the schedule restarts from the day you actually did it.
- One gentle notification on the due day, at the time you choose, with Done
  and Snooze actions right on the notification.
- A streak of days with nothing overdue, a monthly calendar and statistics.
- English and French, switchable in the app.

## Free and Pro

The core app is free, forever, with no ads and no account. Remindwise Pro
(subscription or one-time lifetime purchase) adds: unlimited active
reminders, full statistics with a shareable performance card, unlimited
custom categories, appearance themes and per-task notification times.

## Privacy

No data collection, no tracking, no third-party analytics, no account. Your
data stays on your device and is included in your normal device backup.
Purchases are processed by Apple/Google; we never see payment details.
Full policy: see PRIVACY.md (also hosted at the privacy URL in the store
listing).

## Disclaimer

Task suggestions and default frequencies are general guidance only, not
professional, medical, legal or safety advice. Always follow manufacturer
instructions and the recommendations of qualified professionals (and your
local legal requirements, e.g. vehicle inspections). Remindwise is not
affiliated with any brand or organisation mentioned in task names.
